A Poem About Washington, DC

Monuments and memorials,
Politics and power,
The city of the free,
Washington, DC towers.

Lincoln sits tall,
Reflecting on the past,
The Capitol stands grand,
A symbol built to last.

The Smithsonian breathes culture,
Museums and history,
The Tidal Basin blossoms,
A natural beauty.

The city bustles with life,
Tourists and locals alike,
Washington, DC is a place,
Where freedom truly thrives.
